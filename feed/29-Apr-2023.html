April 28th, 2023

World Premier of "Whatever Excitement Life Brings"


04/29/2023 --

I woke up this morning a completely different person.

For, as of 7:36PM last night, I can say that I'm a peformed composer!


The last couple of months have been so much fun. Working with Dr. Jones and the Ensemble has

offered invaluable moments of learning, challenge, and triumph as a composer.


Composing this work took the better part of my fall '22 semester, and was a harrowing act. It was the

first major work I had ever written; the first time I genuinely put my mind to writing. However, it all paid off

when I shared the "zero-th" edition of Excitement with Dr. Jones. Getting the "seal of approval" from someone

whose exposure to great music eclipses mine tenfold felt like such a relief.


From then on, it was edits and suggestions all day, every day. The polish went on in thick layers, preparing for

rehearsals in March. Despite the pre-rehearsal grind, WAY more work went into the piece during the 6-week rehearsal cycle.

Two entire editions later, with some sections taking completely new forms, it was time!


The premier was surreal. Performing with the group, seeing and hearing all the hard work pay off was so worth everything.

In the aftermath, I felt like a true, real-life composer, and I'm obsessed...


Read the program notes below:


"Whatever Excitement Life Brings is written to represent exactly what the title states. The music is about life - about

the twists and turns, the chances, the successes and failures. It's meant to lead into the future. I conceived the idea for

this piece shortly after a monumentally turbulent time in my life. Three years into undergrad at Wittenberg, I made the bold

choice to give up my studies in Education. I was off to chase the dream, to live my life writing music for people to play,

to listen to, and to love. It was a time of doubt, fear, and unbelievable change. It was the biggest leap-of-faith I had ever

taken. The tunes and ideas in Whatever Excitement Life Brings present what these feelings sound like. Triumphant, majestic

themes soar through the joy of successes and the excitement of new things. Tension brings doubt that leads to fear - fear of

the mysterious tribulations you can expect in life. Ultimately, if you come to embrace and enjoy those thrills and crazy,

fearful moments like I have, you'll feel even more elated when it all works out. So, take the leap, and make the best of it!"
